About us

True health starts with proper foundations. At Chiropractic Foundations, we aim to help society understand this, redefining their interpretation of what true health is.

Our philosophy

Chiropractic Foundations is a chiropractic center located in Ara Damansara, Petaling Jaya. We emphasize on the body's natural ability to heal, without the need of unnecessary surgeries or drug use.

We practice the Gonstead Chiropractic system, which is a series of protocols performed by the chiropractor to every patient. The Gonstead System focuses on being specific and meticulous in finding the root cause of a patient's symptoms, with the help of X-Rays and Temperature Instrumentation (Nervoscope). The goal of a Gonstead Chiropractor is to only correct the specific injured region as opposed to a full body manipulation.
