Common symptoms

Are you experiencing any of these symptoms? We can help.

Lower back pain

Symptoms such as dull or pulling sensation, sharp pain, soreness, difficulties getting up after sitting/waking up caused by a trauma (falls, vehicle accidents), long sitting hours, improper lifting technique.

Neck pain

Symptoms such as pain down to shoulders, pulling sensation, headaches/migraines, stiffness caused by a improper work posture, long sitting hours, excessive phone usage, improper sleeping posture.

Slipped disc / Sciatica

Symptoms such as numbness down the legs, sharp pain, pain with sneezing or coughing, lower back pain caused by a improper lifing technique, trauma (falls, vehicle accidents).

Sports injuries

Symptoms such as ankle sprains, knee sprains, tennis/golfer elbow caused by a direct trauma during physical activity, accumulation of strains over time.
